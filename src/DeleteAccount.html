Request to Delete Your Account

We understand that you may wish to delete your account. Follow the steps below to request the deletion of your account and associated data.

Steps to Delete Your Account

  1. Log in to your account in the app or on our website.
  2. Go to the My Profile section .
  3. Click on Delete Account and follow the on-screen instructions to confirm your request.

If you are unable to access your account, please contact our support team using the link below:

Contact Support

What Happens When You Delete Your Account

When your account is deleted:

  • All your personal data, including your profile information, will be permanently removed from our systems.
  • Any remaining data retention will be handled as per our Privacy Policy.

Please note that some data may be retained for a limited period to comply with legal or regulatory requirements.

Additional Information

For more details about data deletion or to review our policies, visit our Privacy Policy.
